BUSINESS REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT
Project: Customer Account Portal
Version 1.2

1. USER REGISTRATION
The system shall allow new users to register using their email address and a password. Passwords must be at least 8 characters and contain at least one number and one special character. Users must verify their email via a confirmation link before the account is activated.

2. USER LOGIN
Registered users shall be able to log in using their email and password. After 5 failed login attempts, the account shall be locked for 30 minutes. The system shall provide a "Remember Me" option that keeps the user logged in for 30 days.

3. PASSWORD RESET
Users shall be able to reset their password via a link sent to their registered email. The reset link shall expire after 24 hours and be usable only once.

4. PROFILE MANAGEMENT
Users shall be able to update their profile information including name, phone number, and profile picture. Profile pictures must be JPG or PNG format and no larger than 5MB.

5. TRANSACTION HISTORY
Users shall be able to view their transaction history for the past 12 months. The system shall allow users to filter transactions by date range and export the results as a CSV file.

6. NOTIFICATIONS
The system shall send users an email notification for every successful login from a new device. Users shall be able to enable or disable these notifications in their settings.
